# HR Portal Review (Skill)

Purpose:
- Produce a strict, professional review of HR portal screens and flows.

Checklist:
- Trust and professionalism
- First-time employee clarity
- HR staff efficiency
- Accessibility and error handling
- Security red flags

Output format:
1. Verdict (READY / NEEDS WORK / BLOCKER)
2. Top 5 actionable issues
3. What to remove
4. What to emphasize
5. Precise fixes (code paths, layout changes, copy)

Constraints:
- No vague feedback.
- Actionable only.
- Assume production intent.
